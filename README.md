E-Commerce-Operations-And-Sales-Perfprmance-Analysis

Project Overview
This project presents an end-to-end analysis of a large-scale e-commerce dataset containing 120,000+ orders.  
The objective was to uncover key revenue drivers, identify operational inefficiencies, and provide data-driven recommendations to improve profitability and business growth.

 Business Objectives
- Analyze revenue trends and sales performance
- Identify top-performing products and categories
- Detect operational inefficiencies (order cancellations)
- Optimize inventory and logistics strategy.

Tools
Power BI & Power Query→ Data cleaning, transformation (ETL), dashboarding  
Python → Data auditing, missing value handling, EDA  
DAX→ KPI calculations (AOV, Cancellation Rate etc.)

Key Insights
Revenue Performance
Total Revenue: ₹71.77 Million
Peak Sales: ₹26.27M in April 2022

Product Performance
‘Sets’ & ‘Kurtas’ contribute 75% of total volume
 ‘Sets’ alone generate ~50% revenue (₹36M)

Operational Issue
High Cancellation Rate: 14.22%
Indicates major revenue leakage and inefficiency

Geographic Trends
Top markets: Maharashtra, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu
Strong demand for ethnic wear in these regions
 
#Strategic Recommendations

Reduce Cancellations (RTO) 
Implement IVR/SMS verification for high-value orders (>₹1000)

Improve Delivery Speed
Use Amazon FBA for high-demand products

Inventory Optimization
Maintain higher stock for sizes M, L, XL in key regions
